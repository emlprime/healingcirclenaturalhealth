California Legalities

Laws have changed recently in California that don't allow me to use the term Doctor or ND even though that is the degree that I earned. There are a few colleges throughout the United States that train "Medical Naturopaths": they get a 4 year degree and can diagnose, prescribe, perform minor surgery and order medical tests. This group lobbied so that they would exclusively be able to use ND and Doctor as their title. This has left traditional Naturopaths, like myself, in limbo. So though I have earned a Doctorate degree, I cannot use that title.
